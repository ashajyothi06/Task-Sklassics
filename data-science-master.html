Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need any prior experience to join this program?

No prior experience is required. Our program starts with the fundamentals and gradually progresses to advanced topics. However, basic familiarity with programming concepts will be helpful.

How much time should I dedicate weekly?

We recommend dedicating 15-20 hours per week for the mentor-led program. For the self-paced option, you can adjust according to your schedule, but consistency is key.

What kind of projects will I work on?

You'll work on real-world projects including predictive modeling, natural language processing, computer vision, and a comprehensive capstone project using industry datasets.

Is there any job placement assistance?

Yes, the mentor-led program includes resume reviews, interview preparation, and connections to our hiring partners. Many of our students have secured positions at top companies.

Can I pay in installments?

Yes, we offer flexible payment plans. You can pay in 3 monthly installments with no additional fees. Contact our admissions team for more details.
